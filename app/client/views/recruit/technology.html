TECHNOLOGY
We Build Cool Things
Every year, thousands of students across the country apply to our annual HackUCI event on our website. In addition, our website also receives a huge amount of traffic from various companies who visit our website to obtain sponsorship information. By joining the Technology team, you not only get to work on a large-scale hackathon application tracking platform, but you also have the opportunity to build exciting internal tools for our organizers so that they can work more efficiently.
Who We're Looking For
Front-end Developer
As a front-end developer, your primary task is to design and develop an eye-catching user interface that delivers a good first impression to both attendees and sponsors. You will be streamlining the hackathon registration experience and utilize the latest frontend technologies to implement it. What We’re Looking For:
Proficiency in HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript.
Experience in designing and developing clean, modern and responsive UI.
Experience in modern JavaScript frameworks (Angular, React, Vue, jQuery, etc.) and debugging tools (Chrome Dev Tools, etc.).
Ability to write clean, well documented code.
Great learning potential with fast iteration speed.
Back-end Developer
As a back-end developer, you are responsible for developing a robust and secure server-side application to support our client-side web application. You will also be tasked with setting up and maintaining the database that stores our applicants’ data. What We’re Looking For:
Proficiency in JavaScript.
Experience in developing Node.js web applications.
Experience in setting up and maintaining MongoDB databases.
Ability to write clean, well documented code.
Great learning potential with fast iteration speed.
Full-Stack Developer
If you are competent in both front-end and back-end development, this role is for you! You will be working on tasks that span across both the front-end and back-end aspects of the application, from designing and implementing the front-end components to developing the server-side application to working with the database. What We’re Looking For:
Proficiency in HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript.
Experience in designing and developing clean, modern and responsive UI.
Experience in modern JavaScript frameworks (Angular, React, Vue, jQuery, etc.) and debugging tools (Chrome Dev Tools, etc.).
Experience in developing Node.js web applications.
Experience in setting up and maintaining MongoDB databases.
Ability to write clean, well documented code.
Great learning potential with fast iteration speed.
