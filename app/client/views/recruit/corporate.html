CORPORATE OUTREACH
We Form Strategic Alliances
As a Corporate Outreach organizer, you will be responsible for contacting companies for monetary sponsorship, hardware, swag, and mentors. We maintain our relations with existing partners and reach out to other local companies to make HackUCI happen every year. Help us communicate with tech companies by using the following strategies: continuously emailing, scheduling phone calls, attending career fairs, participating at hackathons, and networking at other professional events! Help us grow our professional network so that we can provide more buses, food, and prizes! Motivate students to come to our events by collaborating with top-tier companies.
Who We're Looking For
Hackathon Specialist
Attend other hackathons (LA Hacks, SD Hacks, Cal Hacks, Hacktech, Citrus Hack, TreeHacks, etc) and talk to the sponsors there. Your job is to advertise HackUCI, network with companies, collect contact information, record what happened, and continuously follow up with the people you've met.
Career Fair Specialist
Attend and volunteer at UCI career fairs (Fall Career Fair, STEM Career Fair, etc). Your job is to advertise HackUCI, network with companies, collect contact information, record what happened, and continuously follow up with the people you've met.
Workshop Specialist
Attend company-hosted workshops at or near UCI. Your job is to advertise HackUCI, network with companies, collect contact information, record what happened, and continuously follow up with the people you've met.
Treasurer
Create sponsorship agreement forms and invoices. Help document our finances and large sponsorship orders. Manage our point-of-sale system and club accounts.
