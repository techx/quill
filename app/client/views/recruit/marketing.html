MARKETING
We Make Ourselves Known
Marketing is in charge of building the bridge between the club and the public audience. Being a part of marketing means making sure everyone hears our announcements and updates about our events. This includes drafting emails, posting on social media platforms, writing blogs for the club website, and much more. As a result, maintaining public relations is extremely important and having great communication skills is imperative.
Who We're Looking For
General Marketing Organizer
As a general marketing organizer, your responsibilities vary from sending emails via Mailchimp to making posts on social media platforms in a witty and creative manner. Additionally, you will assist in planning workshops that occur throughout the school year. You are required to meet strict deadlines and have exceptional communication skills since marketing is all about reaching out to the public. If you have exceptional photography skills, let us know if you are interested in taking photos to assist in future marketing materials on top of your daily marketing tasks.
Graphic Designer
Marketing can be described as the face of the company and that holds true for our club. As a graphic designer, you will be responsible for creating graphics for various platforms such as Facebook or our club website. Graphic designs will follow general guidelines and have to be submitted before given deadlines. You must also have a keen attention to detail and have a proficient knowledge in visual design, color theory, and typography. Knowledge of Photoshop or some professional grade software is required as well.
